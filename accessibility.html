Accessibility

Everyone deserves clear, usable mortgage math.

We aim to meet or exceed WCAG 2.2 AA where feasible. The app favors high contrast, keyboard navigation, clear focus states, and reduced motion. Charts include text equivalents and color-independent legends.

Current efforts

  • Semantic HTML, labeled form controls, keyboard focus order.
  • Color contrast using emerald / gold palette; non-color indicators in legends.
  • Mobile-first layout and larger hit targets for tap controls.
  • Accessible names for calculator keys (screen-reader friendly).

Known areas we’re improving

  • Providing additional aria-live updates for certain computed results.
  • Adding “Skip to content” link on the main tool page.

Request assistance

If you experience a barrier, please email me@sabrinamontalvo.com with the page URL, your device/browser, and what didn’t work. We’ll reply and work on a fix.
