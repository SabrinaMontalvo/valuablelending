Privacy Notice

We keep things simple and respectful.

Plain-English summary: We don’t sell your personal information. We don’t use trackers or analytics that identify you across sites. If you email us, we’ll use your message only to respond and support you.

What we collect

  • Calculator inputs: Your numbers are processed in your browser. We do not store them on our servers.
  • Basic logs: Our hosting provider may capture standard server logs (e.g., IP address, user agent) for security and reliability.
  • Messages: If you contact us, we’ll receive the info you send (like your email address and message).

How we use information

  • To provide and improve the Service.
  • To respond to your requests and support needs.
  • To detect, prevent, or address security and abuse.

Cookies & analytics

We aim to avoid invasive tracking. If we add privacy-friendly analytics in the future, we’ll update this page and keep it aggregate-only.

Your choices

  • You can use the tools without creating an account.
  • You can request we delete emails you’ve sent us: privacy@valuablelending.com.

Data location & retention

The Service is hosted in the United States. Support emails are retained only as long as needed for support and legal compliance.

Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal data.

Changes

We may update this notice over time. We will post the new date when we do.
